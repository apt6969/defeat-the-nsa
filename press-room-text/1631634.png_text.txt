PRESS RELEASE | Feb. 24, 2009

NSA To Participate in the First
Intelligence Community Virtual
Career Fair

[he National Security Agency is hosting its own virtual career fair today
Tuesday February 24 from 3pm-8pm EST. This is an online event for job
seekers that will take place in an interactive 3-D environment.

While logged on to the event candidates will be able to visit different
virtual career field booths to:

¢ chat with NSA recruiters;

e learn about what NSA positions are available;
¢ apply to open positions;
¢ watch a presentation about NSA; and
¢ participate in a live Q&A session.

There are currently more than 7 000 people registered to attend this
event. If you are interested in participating please register online by
visiting www.NSAvirtualfair.com.

If you are a media outlet and would like additional information or to
schedule an interview about the career fair or NSA recruitment efforts
please contact NSA Public and Media Affairs via phone 301-688-6524
and/or email via nsapao@nsa.gov.

For more information regarding NSA please visit NSA’s website NSA. gov.
