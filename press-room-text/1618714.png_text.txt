PRESS RELEASE VIEW

PRESS RELEASE | April 18, 2014

National Security Agency, Civil
Liberties and Privacy Office Report

provides an unclassified overview of NSA‘s
implementation of Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act Section 702. It is
also entered into the Federal Register ( ; docket
PCLOB-2013-005-0073) to satisfy PCLOB request for information to
inform their upcoming report and to be more transparent to the public.
The report is based primarily on previously approved and declassified
material related FISA Section 702 and adds explanations of NSA‘s privacy
and civil liberties protections currently in place.
