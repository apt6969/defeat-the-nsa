PRESS RELEASE | July 27, 2018

NSA Statement on Semi-Annual
Office of the Inspector General
Report to Congress

FORT MEADE, Md. â€”

NSA welcomes the semi-annual report of the Office of the Inspector
General (OIG) and OIG's efforts to make the unclassified version as
complete and transparent as possible for the public. We have always
encouraged our workforce to cooperate fully with the OIG's independent
efforts to assess our programs, operations and activities to ensure
compliance with the law and consistency with protecting civil liberties
and privacy. Agency management agreed with all of the OIG's
recommendations and is committed to working with the OIG to ensure
those recommendations are implemented.
